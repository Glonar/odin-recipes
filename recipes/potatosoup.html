Potato Soup

A delicious potato soup that is both easy to make, and easy on the pocketbook.

Ingredients

  • 6 strips uncooked bacon chopped into small peices
  • 3 Tablespoons of butter
  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • 3 large garlic cloves
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 lbs gold potatoes (peeled and diced)
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2/3 cups of heavy cream
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 1/4 - 1/2 teaspoon ancho chili powder
  • 2/3 cup sour cream
  • Shredded cheese, chives, and additional sour cream and bacon for topping
